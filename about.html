Historically, Qassomar Investment

In the beginning, Our father was a servant. In 1993 he decided to grow tobacco. After harvesting and selling, he decided to go to Mangochi town to do business.

After a while, the people of the town began to trust his shop. At that time the shop was called LIMBIKANI. Due to some problemes, the business was disrupted. He started looking for money again, trying this and that so that he might get a chance to sell again. Then Allah softened him and he got another capital and started doing business again. Since then the shop came back to be trusted especially tailoring products. In the year 2023 is when the name Qassomar Investement begins.

This name came especially in praise of Allah and in realizing that this was not only done because of our wisdom or hard work, but it is a blessing and grace from Allah.
